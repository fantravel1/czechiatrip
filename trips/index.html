2–3 Days: Prague + One Escape

Most visitors only do Prague. Make the most of a short trip: experience the city's heart, then escape to a castle, a forest walk, or a beer culture evening in a neighborhood the tourists miss. One strategic day trip changes everything.

5–7 Days: Prague + Countryside Loop

The sweet spot. Start in Prague, loop through a spa town, explore a castle region, walk a nature corridor, and discover a folk village cluster. This is where Czechia starts revealing itself. Public transport covers it all.

10–14 Days: Full Czechia Immersion

Prague + Moravia + Bohemia in a grand loop. Multi-city routing through deep countryside, modern design cities like Brno, wine regions, rock formations, and the complete Czech experience. This is how you do it properly.
